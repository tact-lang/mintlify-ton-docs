**TON Connect AppKit** is an open-source collection of supplementary SDKs that help integrate your dApp with TON.

**AppKit includes:**
- Connection and related UIs (buttons, modals, etc.)
- Transaction UX flows (send Toncoin, check balanced, track transactions, sign data, request proofs)
- SDKs for various frameworks and environments
