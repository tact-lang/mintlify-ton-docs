This guide helps you integrate your custodial or non-custodial wallet with TON or partially build a basic fresh one from scratch with the help of [TON Connect](/ecosystem/ton-connect) and [WalletKit](/ecosystem/ton-connect/walletkit).

TON Connect is a standard wallet connection protocol used on TON. It consists of many supplementary SDKs and supervises two major use-cases: integrations of dApps with TON and custom wallet integrations. The latter are done via the WalletKit.
