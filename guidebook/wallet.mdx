Integrate a custodial or non-custodial wallet with [The Open Network (TON)](/ton/glossary) using [TON Connect](/ecosystem/ton-connect) and [WalletKit](/ecosystem/ton-connect/walletkit), or build a basic wallet from scratch.

TON Connect is a standard wallet connection protocol used on TON. It consists of many supplementary software development kits (SDKs) and covers two major use cases: integrations of decentralized applications (dApps) with TON and custom wallet integrations. The latter use WalletKit.
