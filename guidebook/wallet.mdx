This guide helps you integrate your custodial or non-custodial wallet with TON or partially build a basic fresh one from scratch with the help of [TON Connect](/ecosystem/ton-connect) and auxiliary libraries.

TON Connect is a standard wallet connection protocol used on TON. It consists of many supplementary SDKs, which are split into two groups: ones that help integrate your dApp with TON ([AppKit](/ecosystem/ton-connect/appkit)), and ones that help integrate your wallet ([WalletKit](/ecosystem/ton-connect/appkit)).

Thus, this guide shows how to use TON Connect _WalletKit_ libraries to integrate the TON Blockchain into your dApp. To proceed, select your framework or environment:
